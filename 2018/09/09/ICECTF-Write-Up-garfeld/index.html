ICECTF - garfeld

Posted on 2018-09-09

Challenge

You found the marketing campaign for a brand new sitcom. Garfeld! It has a secret message engraved on it. Do you think you can figure out what they’re trying to say?

[100 points]

Initial thoughts

We are given this image :

We can clearly see that on the bottom is the encrypted flag, and in the top-right corner is what seems to be a decryption key.

I immediately thought about some kind of Caesar cipher, but letter by letter, using the special key we had.

So the first letter wouldn’t change, the second would go up (or down) 7 times, the third 2 times, etc.

I took a sheet of paper and tried my theory on the first six characters, as I already knew what the output should give : ICECTF.

And, guess what?

Now let’s craft the loop that will go through each of enc’s letters and move them down according to the key we were given.

Once the end of the key is reached, let’s go back to its beginning.

Oh, and if our script encounters any odd characters like {, } or _ (as we can already see in the encrypted flag), let’s just add them to our liste without trying to change them and without skipping to the key’s next number.

Looks good enough. Finally, let’s join our list’s characters and print them out in uppercase.

After thoughts

It’s always a good feeling when I’m able to figure out something on my own, write the script to automate it and see that it works in the end.

To be honest, I could have just done it by hand, but it’d have taken me much more time than writing a script. And it’d have been far less interesting.
